Our founder

The heart behind Mankuke

Built from passion, qualification, and deep community love.

MM
Mrs Moremi Malero Evelyn
Principal, Founder & Director — Mankuke Day Care

Mrs Moremi Malero Evelyn founded Mankuke Day Care in 2020 with a vision to create a nurturing, safe, and professionally run early childhood centre that every family in Ga-Moropo and Neighbouring Villages could trust. Her dedication to children goes far beyond her qualifications — it is rooted in genuine love and a belief that every child deserves the best possible start in life.

Under her leadership, Mankuke has grown into a registered NPO, earned DBE Bronze Certification through the Bana Pele Program, and secured official land status through the Traditional Council.
